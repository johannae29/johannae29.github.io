About

I am an UX Designer with a positive mind and attention to details with a background as a software tester. I strive to explore each user journey by listening and learning from the users and then create an easy and meaningful experience through curiosity and empathy. I am structured, thorough and used to working on different projects and I enjoy keeping track of many different things at the same time.


I live in Karlskrona with my husband and two daughters. I love to go on adventures to explore the world, both near and far. In my free time I like to do yoga, spend time in the garden and to do puzzles.
