About

I am an UX Design student with a positive mind and attention to details with a background as a software tester. I strive to explore each user journey by listening and learning from the users and then create an easy and meaningful experience through curiosity and empathy. I am structured, thorough and used to working on different projects at the same time from my time as a software tester, which means that I often become a bit like the "spider in the web" as I enjoy keeping track of many different things at the same time.


I live in Karlskrona with my husband and two daughters. I love to go on adventures to explore the world, both close and far. In my free time I like to do yoga, spend time in the garden and to do puzzles.

Resume

I am a driven software tester with many years of experience of agile work in the industry who is now retraining as a UX Designer. As a software tester, I have realized the importance of user experience and that the process of creating good software must start early. I have always wanted to deliver a product with high quality, but to make the customer happy, not only testing is needed but also a thorough UX design to understand the user's needs.
